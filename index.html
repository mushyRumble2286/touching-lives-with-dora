About Dora

Touching Lives with Dora & Friends is a transformative community service initiative dedicated to empowering students in suburban areas. Founded by Dora Eni-Ibukun Adeagbo, this project focuses on providing essential educational resources while instilling positive values that inspire young minds to dream beyond their current circumstances.

The initiative primarily serves students in government-owned schools, addressing the immediate needs that often prevent children from fully engaging in their education. By removing these barriers, we create an environment where students can focus on learning and personal growth.

Our Mission

Inspire Young Minds

Teaching positive values and expanding horizons beyond the immediate suburban environment.

Remove Barriers

Providing essential school supplies so students can focus on learning without worry.

Opening doors to a brighter future through education and mentorship.

Dora's Project

Our comprehensive community service project puts smiles on the faces of students and helps them stay eager to learn. Most kids in the suburbs can't afford these basic necessities, and we're committed to changing that.
